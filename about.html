Our Mission

Welcome to the UMD Bulldogs in Technology Club, where we're passionate about boosting knowledge and skills in IT and technology. Our club welcomes all, whether you're looking to boost your career, advance your academic journey, or simply want to dive deeper into the world of technology and AI. We regularly host guest speakers who provide valuable insights into the real-world use of technology and analytics. We offer events that assist in resume building and career development to help you succeed in your professional pursuits. Beyond that, we also organize club bonding events, creating a supportive community where lasting connections are forged.
